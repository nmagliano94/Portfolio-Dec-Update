What I Do

HTML Email Development

Build responsive HTML email templates for clients/marketing teams. Making campaign ideas come to life by working closely with designers and marketers to ensure the emails display and function correctly so that subscribers can engage with it properly.

Email Testing

Thorough testing done to make sure the email delivers high-quality, visually appealing, and functional campaigns. Testing through a service like Litmus ensures the email is rendering and functioning properly across all the most popular clients and devices.

Landing Pages

Build a standalone web page that contacts/potential contacts can visit after clicking on a shared link in an email, capturing leads for your business. Made to be responsive across all devices and work properly on all major clients.
